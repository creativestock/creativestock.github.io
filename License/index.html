a rule of thumb (tldr)

If you are working on something and want to use illustrations to improve its appearance, modified or not, without the need for attribution or cost, you are good to go. If you find creativestock or its illustrations to be in the center of what you are doing (e.g. sell or re-distribute one/some of them, add them in an app), then you probably should not proceed.
